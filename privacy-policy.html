Privacy Policy

At HILOFIX, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This site is designed to be as minimal and privacy-friendly as possible.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how our website is performing (e.g., measuring page views and load times). This service is entirely privacy-first:

  • No Cookies: It does not use any client-side state, such as cookies or localStorage, to collect usage metrics.
  • No Personal Data: It does not collect or store IP addresses or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Fingerprinting: It does not track you across other websites or build a profile of your behavior.

Contact Information

If you choose to contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp, we will only use the information you provide (such as your phone number or email address) to respond to your specific inquiry regarding our refurbished laptops. We do not sell or share your contact details with third parties for marketing purposes.

Please note that communicating via WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy.
